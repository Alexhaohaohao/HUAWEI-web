Huawei ICT Academy

Global School-Enterprise Cooperation Project

The Huawei ICT Academy, led by Huawei, is a global school-enterprise
cooperation project aimed at delivering Huawei ICT technology and product knowledge to university students worldwide, encouraging them to participate in Huawei vocational certification, and cultivating innovative and application-oriented technical talents for society and the ICT industry chain globally.

Learn More Find ICT Academy

Our Advantages

Global Certification

Develop together with partners, share resources globally, and build a leading ICT certification system and a healthy talent ecosystem.

Rich Resources

The Huawei ICT Academy has developed 22 technical directions and certifications, as well as 14 professional courses, with over 2670 Huawei certified teachers providing instruction.

Supporting Services

Supporting services such as Huawei teacher certification, student certification, teaching materials, and laboratory equipment solutions.

Priority Employment

Every year, dozens of dual selection fairs are held globally, with over 1000 recruitment companies cooperating with Huawei, offering nearly a thousand job positions preferentially.
